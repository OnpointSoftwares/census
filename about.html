About

Welcome to the Census System. Our goal is to collect, analyze, and disseminate accurate population data for better planning and development.

Our Mission

To provide reliable and timely demographic data that supports national planning and policy-making.

Our Vision

To be a leading provider of demographic data, fostering informed decision-making for sustainable development.

What We Do

  • Conduct national censuses and surveys.
  • Analyze demographic trends and patterns.
  • Provide data for policy formulation and planning.
  • Ensure data privacy and security.
